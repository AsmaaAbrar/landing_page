What is EduMark?

EduMark is a free web service for schools and universities that aims to grading assignments in a paperless way .The primary purpose of EduMark is to streamline the process of sharing mark and lecture between teacher and student .

Who use the system

A person managing the whole learning process.

See the percentage of success and fail throw a simple column chart.
Download mark table as pdf file.
